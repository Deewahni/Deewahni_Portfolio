Health Benefits

Studies suggest that durian fruit offers the following health benefits (2Trusted Source, 3Trusted Source): Reduces cancer risk. Its antioxidants may neutralize cancer-promoting free radicals. In one test-tube study, durian extract prevented a strain of breast cancer cells from spreading. Prevents heart disease. Fights infection. The rind contains compounds that have antibacterial and anti-yeast properties. Durian has a lower glycemic index (GI) than many other tropical fruits, meaning it may spike blood sugar levels less.
